The Tracker

The tracker is the core of the Sky Hub system. The tracker is responsible for processing sensor and video feed using AI algorithms. It will detect the motion or anomolous sensor events and trigger a recording processing. The tracker will record this event and send the data associated with the event into the cloud. The cloud will examine the event, and alert other trackers in the area to look for localized event.
